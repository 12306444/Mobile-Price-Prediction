How This AI Model Works

This advanced machine learning model analyzes 20 technical specifications to predict the price category of mobile phones. The model was trained on thousands of mobile phone datasets and uses ensemble algorithms to identify complex patterns and correlations between specifications and pricing.

Key factors like RAM, battery power, camera quality, screen resolution, and processing power are weighted according to their impact on the final price. Higher values in these specifications typically result in higher price categories.

The model uses a gradient boosting algorithm that combines multiple weak predictors to create a strong predictive model with high accuracy and generalization capabilities.
